常见问题解答 (FAQ)

Q: 如何连接我的雨水收集设备？

A: 在应用首页，点击"添加设备"，按照向导步骤操作：

  1. 选择设备类型
  2. 确保设备处于配对模式（长按设备上的配对按钮5秒）
  3. 将手机连接到设备创建的Wi-Fi网络
  4. 返回应用完成配置
Q: 数据不同步怎么办？

A: 如果数据不同步，请尝试以下步骤：

  1. 确保设备和手机都已连接网络
  2. 在应用中下拉刷新数据
  3. 检查设备是否在线（设备页面会显示设备状态）
  4. 重启设备和应用
  5. 如问题依然存在，请联系客服
Q: 如何取消订阅？

A: 您可以通过以下步骤取消订阅：

  1. 打开App Store
  2. 点击您的头像图标
  3. 选择"订阅"
  4. 找到"煜德云枢-雨水智管家"
  5. 点击"取消订阅"

取消后，您可以继续使用服务直到当前订阅周期结束。

Q: 我可以在多个设备上使用同一账号吗？

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I connect my rainwater collection device?

A: On the app home page, click "Add Device" and follow the wizard steps:

  1. Select the device type
  2. Ensure the device is in pairing mode (press and hold the pairing button on the device for 5 seconds)
  3. Connect your phone to the Wi-Fi network created by the device
  4. Return to the app to complete the configuration
Q: What should I do if data doesn't synchronize?

A: If data doesn't synchronize, please try the following steps:

  1. Ensure both the device and phone are connected to the network
  2. Pull down to refresh data in the app
  3. Check if the device is online (the device page will show the device status)
  4. Restart the device and the app
  5. If the problem persists, please contact customer service
Q: How do I cancel my subscription?

A: You can cancel your subscription through the following steps:

  1. Open the App Store
  2. Tap your profile icon
  3. Select "Subscriptions"
  4. Find "YuDe Cloud Hub - Rain Water Manager"
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

After cancellation, you can continue using the service until the end of the current subscription period.

Q: Can I use the same account on multiple devices?

A: Yes, you can log in with the same account on multiple devices, and the data will be automatically synchronized.
